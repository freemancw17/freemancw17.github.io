Why Clean Water is Essential

Clean Water is an essential resourse for us because humans cannot go more than 3-4 days without it. Even if you do continue to breathe after this time, you will go into shock and become vegetative. It's also essential to protect us from the many diseases around the world. This is why the fact that billions of people around the world don't have access to Clean Water is so horrific.

Facts about Clean Water

- 25% of health care facilities lack basic water services.
- 30% of people lack access to safely managed drinking water services.
- 785 million people lack even a basic drinking-water service.
- 144 million people are dependent on surface water to drink from.
- Contaminated drinking water is estimated to cause 485 000 diarrhoeal deaths each year.
