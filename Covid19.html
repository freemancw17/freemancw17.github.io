What Covid-19 taught us about the importance of Clean Water and Sanitation

The Covid-19 pandemic showed everybody the true importance of having access, and taking advantage of Clean Drinking Water and Sanitation Facilities. One of, if not the best way, to combat the spread of the deadly virus, is to keep on top of sanitation practices such as hand washing.

Impacts of Covid-19 on people without Clean Water and Sanitation

The Impacts of Covid-19 could be considerably higher on people anble to get access to Clean Water and Sanitation. This is because as mentioned earlier, sanitation practices such as hand washing are a crucial part of limiting the spread of Covid-19.
